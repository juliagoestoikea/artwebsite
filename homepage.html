About Me

My name is Julia, and I'm a student studying Physics and Archaeology at Columbia University. I'm originally from San Francisco. In my free time, I love making art, so I decided to share it here. I work with all sorts of mediums, but most of my art is centered around memory. I look to understand how we make our memories and preserve them. I like to use art to synthetically create the methods that we use to preserve and create memories. I also make art with bacteria for Danino Studios whose work can be found here. I also tattoo, and all of that info can be found on my instagram. Hope you enjoy and please feel free to reach out!
